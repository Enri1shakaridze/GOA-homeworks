The GOA Hackathon will take place in Tbilisi, at the Rustavi 2 building on the 14th floor, from 16:00 to 21:00 on May 11th.
Students will be divided into groups, and each group will have a squad leader.
There will also be a competition in exercises (agimanies).
It's a fun programming challenge where you'll learn a lot, meet new friends, and have a great time.
