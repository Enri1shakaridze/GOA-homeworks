About Football

Football, also known as soccer, is a sport that brings people together. It is played by two teams of eleven players, and the objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net.

Fun Fact

Did you know that football is the most popular sport in the world?

History of Football

The history of football dates back to the 19th century, and it has evolved into the global phenomenon we know today.
