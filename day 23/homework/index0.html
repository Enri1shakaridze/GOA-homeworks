Sea Animals Encyclopedia

Explore the fascinating world under the sea

Dolphin

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and complex communication. They are part of the toothed whale family and can be found all over the world's oceans. Dolphins use echolocation to navigate and hunt prey.

Octopus

The octopus is a soft-bodied, eight-limbed mollusk known for its intelligence and ability to camouflage. They live in various marine habitats, from shallow waters to deep oceans. Octopuses can squeeze through tiny gaps and even open jars!

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles are long-living reptiles that have roamed the oceans for millions of years. They migrate long distances between feeding grounds and nesting beaches. Threats include plastic pollution, fishing nets, and habitat destruction.
